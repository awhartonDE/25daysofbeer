Beer Advent for the Holidays


2015

December 1 Samuel Smith's Oatmeal Stout


Details: Brewed with well water (the original well at the Old Brewery, sunk in 1758, is still in use, with the hard well water being drawn from 85 feet underground); fermented in ‘stone Yorkshire squares’ to create an almost opaque, wonderfully silky and smooth textured ale with a complex medium dry palate and bittersweet finish.

Style: Oatmeal Stout

BA Score: 94

ABV: 5.0%

Notes: Not too much head. Very creamy texture, sweet flavor. Nice dark color for a stout. Goes down very nicely.

December 2 Aecht Schlenkerla Rauchbier Märzen


Details: Aecht Schlenkerla Rauchbier Maerzen is unpasteurized and made entirely from smoked malt. Heller Trum is one of the very few breweries left in Germany that does its own malting. During germination of barley, the smoke of beech wood fire is "forced" through the germinating barley for a total of between 24 and 36 hours at varying temperatures; therefore, Aecht Schlenkerla Rauchbier gains an unusual smoky dryness from this process.

Style: Rauchbier

BA Score: 91

ABV: 5.4%

Notes: Very smokey flavor, but not too much. First taste kind of shocks you, but it gets better with each drink. Not too bad of a finish, either.
